My Concert Experiences

Twenty One Pilots Emotional Roadshow - New Orleans

On March 2, 2017 I went to see Twenty One Pilots for the first time ever! I was so excited because Twenty One Pilots are one of my favorite bands and it was my very first time going to a actual concert. They have helped me through the darkest times in my life. I was so grateful to be able to see them. That night of the concert I cried happy tears and had so much fun. The opening bands were Judah & The Lion and Jon Bellion & Travis Mendes. They were amazing! During the middle of the show, Jon and Travis & Judah and The Loin, joined Twenty One Pilots to cover a few songs. They put on an amazing show and I will never forget it.
